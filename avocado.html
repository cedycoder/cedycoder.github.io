¡Hola! I'm Carlos the Avocado

Meet Carlos, the happiest avocado from Mexico! He loves wearing his colorful sombrero, dancing to mariachi music, and of course, being delicious in tacos and guacamole.

Did you know? Mexico produces about 30% of the world's avocados, mostly in the state of Michoacán.
